About 40K Army Cleaner

A powerful tool for cleaning and formatting Warhammer 40K army rosters

Version 1.0.0

What It Does

1

Cleans Roster Formatting

Removes redundant formatting, section headers, and unnecessary whitespace from Games Workshop roster exports.

2

Smart Unit Formatting

Applies faction-specific formatting to unit names, making them more readable and consistent.

3

Customizable Options

Control what information to display: points, model counts, headers, and various formatting options.

4

Progressive Web App

Works offline, can be installed on your device, and provides a native app-like experience.

Key Features

Core functionality

  • Drag and drop roster files
  • One-click copy to clipboard
  • Discord-friendly export
  • Works offline

User experience & privacy

  • Mobile-friendly design
  • No data sent to servers
  • No ads nor tracking
  • Open source
